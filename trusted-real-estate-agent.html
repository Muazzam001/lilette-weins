Whether you’re thinking about buying or selling, finding a trusted real estate agent in a sea of over 40,000 professionals isn’t the easiest task. So, what makes me different from the rest? Well…

To me, this is more than a transaction.

Am I going to work my hardest to get you the best possible price? Absolutely! But I’m also just as focused on how you’re feeling at every stage.

Buying and selling are emotional experiences, and as a real estate agent, I’ll be your trusted advisor.

You'll always get 100% truth from me.

As your partner in real estate, I’ll always tell it like it is. I can’t sleep if I know my client isn’t making the best choice, so whether I’m helping you see a home’s overlooked flaws or uncover its exciting potential, you’ll only receive upfront and honest service.

You'll get to know homes inside and out.

Whether you’re looking for a home or selling your own, it’s all about the make-or-break details. My experience renovating a 100-year-old house taught me how to find significant issues that should be flagged and identify minor issues and how to improve them.

You'll have the help of a local specialist.

West Toronto is my home and the area that I know best. I grew up here, volunteer here, and am now raising my own kids here. But that’s not where my expertise ends! I’ve worked everywhere from The Beach to Mississauga, Burlington, Oakville, and across the GTA.

Your experience will be handled with care.

This is especially true in more sensitive situations, such as selling in the midst of separation. With my experience, this process will be completed with thoughtfulness, understanding, and a streamlined approach that helps everything run smoothly.
